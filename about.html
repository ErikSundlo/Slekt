About This Archive

My father Harald Sundlo spent his retirement transcribing his own father's scribblings — notes on family history, life in the Caucasus in 1919, and the Eastern Front during the Second World War. I digitised and organised what he left behind, and put it online rather than let it moulder in a drawer.

The English translations were produced with the assistance of Claude Code. The results are what one might charitably call "reasonable fidelity with character."
